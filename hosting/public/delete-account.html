Request Data Deletion

In compliance with Google Play and App Store policies, you can request the deletion of your UniHub account and all associated data through this form.

How it works:

  • We will verify your identity via email.
  • Once verified, all your profile info, listings, and messages will be permanently removed.
  • This process typically takes 24-48 hours.

To request the deletion of your account and all associated data, please fill out our official request form:


Open Data Deletion Form

Note: If you still have access to the app, you can delete your account instantly from Settings > Delete Account.
