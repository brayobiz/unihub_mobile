At UniHub, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us when you create an account, update your profile, post listings, or communicate with other users.

  • Account Information: Name, email address, gender, and authentication data (including Google Sign-In details).
  • Profile Information: Profile pictures, university affiliation, campus, course of study, year of study, biography, skills, and interests.
  • Verification Data: For trust and safety, we collect images of your Student ID, Government-issued ID, and "selfie" photographs to verify your identity and university enrollment.
  • Location Data: We collect campus and university information to show you relevant marketplace, housing, and event listings in your specific area.
  • User-Generated Content: Marketplace listings, housing advertisements, shared academic notes (PDFs/Documents), events organized, gig postings/applications, anonymous confessions, messages sent through our chat feature, and all uploaded images.
  • Technical Data: Device information, IP addresses, FCM tokens (for notifications), and usage patterns collected through Firebase Services and Cloudinary.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for various purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the UniHub platform.
  • To facilitate marketplace transactions and housing connections.
  • To enable private messaging and support communications.
  • To send push notifications regarding account activity and community updates.
  • To verify student status and issue verification badges.
  • To monitor and moderate content (including anonymous confessions) to ensure community safety.
  • To calculate and display your "Trust Score" based on successful interactions and verifications.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored and processed using Firebase (a Google service). We implement industry-standard security measures provided by Firebase, including encrypted data storage and secure transit (SSL/TLS), to protect your personal information. Images and documents are securely handled via Cloudinary.

4. User-Generated Content & Anonymity

Information you post in the Marketplace, Housing, Events, or Notes sections is visible to other UniHub users. Confessions are designed to be anonymous to other users, but are linked to your account ID for moderation purposes (e.g., to prevent abuse). Private messages are intended to be private between the participants but may be reviewed by administrators in response to reports of misconduct.

5. Your Rights & Data Portability

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Data Access & Export: You can download a copy of all your personal data directly from the "Settings" menu within the UniHub app. This includes your profile, listings, posts, and chat history in a machine-readable JSON format.
  • Account Deletion: You have the right to delete your account at any time through the application settings. Upon deletion, your personal profile information and all of your active listings are permanently removed from our active databases.

Note on Communications: Messages you have sent to other users will remain in their message history to maintain their conversation records, but your identity will be displayed as "Deleted User".

6. Third-Party Services

UniHub utilizes the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): For authentication, database hosting (Firestore), analytics, and push notifications.
  • Cloudinary: For secure storage and delivery of images and documents.
  • Google Sign-In: For account authentication.

We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of these providers: Google Privacy Policy.

7. Children's Privacy

UniHub is intended for university students (18 years and older) and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under 18. If you are under 18, you are not permitted to use UniHub.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our Support Form.
