At UniHub, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us when you create an account, update your profile, post listings, or communicate with other users.

  • Account Information: Name, email address, gender, and authentication data (including Google Sign-In details).
  • Profile Information: Profile pictures, university affiliation, course of study, biography, skills, and interests.
  • Verification Data: For trust and safety, we may collect images of your Student ID, Government-issued ID, and "selfie" photographs to verify your identity and university enrollment.
  • Location Data: We collect campus and university information to show you relevant marketplace and housing listings in your specific area.
  • User-Generated Content: Marketplace listings, housing advertisements, shared academic notes (PDFs/Documents), messages sent through our chat feature, and all uploaded images.
  • Technical Data: Device information, IP addresses, and usage patterns collected through Firebase Services.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for various purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the UniHub platform.
  • To facilitate marketplace transactions and housing connections.
  • To enable private messaging and support communications.
  • To send push notifications regarding account activity and updates.
  • To verify student status and issue verification badges.
  • To monitor and moderate content to ensure community safety.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored and processed using Firebase (a Google service). We implement industry-standard security measures provided by Firebase, including encrypted data storage and secure transit (SSL/TLS), to protect your personal information.

4. User-Generated Content

Please be aware that information you post in the Marketplace, Housing, or Notes sharing sections is visible to other UniHub users. Private messages are intended to be private between the participants but may be reviewed by administrators in response to reports of misconduct.

5. Account Deletion & Data Retention

You have the right to delete your account at any time through the application settings. Upon deletion, your personal profile information and all of your active Marketplace listings, Housing advertisements, and shared Academic Notes are permanently removed from our active databases.

Note on Communications: Messages you have sent to other users will remain in their message history, but your identity will be displayed as "Deleted User" to maintain their conversation records.

To request full data erasure via web, please submit a request through our Support & Data Request Form.

6. Third-Party Services

UniHub utilizes the following third-party services to provide our platform:

  • Firebase (Google): For authentication, database hosting (Firestore), and push notifications.
  • Cloudinary: For secure storage and delivery of all user-uploaded images, documents, and verification files.
  • Google Sign-In: For account creation and authentication.

We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of these providers: Google Privacy Policy.

7. Children's Privacy

UniHub is intended for university students and is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our Support Form.
